ABOUT

A data analyst driven by curiosity and resilience, helping organizations transform data into actionable insights for real-world impact.

Just a curious young man from a small village in Ghana who found his passion in data and discovery. Growing up without access to a computer, I completed junior high school having only seen computers drawn on a blackboard. But that did not stop me — I was determined to learn, grow, and change my story.

Today, I use data to uncover patterns, communicate insight, and support better decision-making. I work with Python, Excel, R, SQL, Power BI, and QGIS, and I’ve contributed to projects involving predictive modeling, real estate valuation, and spatial optimization.

My mission is simple: to use data to solve real-world problems — from housing equity and business strategy to policy and smarter cities. I believe data can help build fairer outcomes, and I’m committed to doing work that matters.
