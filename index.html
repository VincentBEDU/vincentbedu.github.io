ABOUT

A Data Scientist/Analyst driven by curiosity and resilience, helping organizations transform data into actionable insights for real-world impact.

I’m Vincent, a Data Scientist/Analyst and real estate professional with a Master’s in Urban Spatial Science from University College London and a background in Real Estate. Raised in a small village in Ghana, I discovered my passion for data and discovery despite growing up without access to a computer, a journey that shaped my resilience, curiosity, and commitment to learning. Today, I use data to turn complex problems into practical, real-world insights.

My work spans data analysis, spatial analytics, and predictive modeling, using tools like Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, and QGIS. I build clear dashboards, automate reporting, and translate data into decisions teams can trust.

My mission is simple: to use data to solve real-world problems across real estate, urban systems, and business decision-making, from housing markets and investment strategy to policy and smarter cities. I believe insight should lead to action, and I’m committed to doing work that matters.
